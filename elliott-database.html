🎯 The Ideal Elliott Nominee

  • Background: Former C-suite executive (CEO, CFO, COO) at Fortune 500 company
  • Top Education: Wharton MBA appears most frequently, followed by other top-10 business schools
  • Expertise: Deep sector knowledge; operators with execution experience, not financial engineers
  • Sweet Spot: 2-3 board seats per campaign drives optimal alignment without distraction
  • Timeline: 18-36 month holding period maximizes value capture and operational improvements
  • Best Outcomes: Technology (take-private/strategic sales), Energy (operational restructuring)
